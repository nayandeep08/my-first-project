Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and then mix in the egg mixture.
  5. Serve hot with extra parmesan.
